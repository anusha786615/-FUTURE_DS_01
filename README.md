# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics

### Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship (2026)



## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to identify revenue trends, profitable products, customer purchasing behavior, and regional performance.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow, including:

* Data Cleaning
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* KPI Analysis
* Dashboard Development
* Business Storytelling
* Recommendation Generation



## Objectives

The objectives of this project are:

* Clean and prepare raw sales data.
* Analyze business performance through KPIs.
* Identify revenue trends over time.
* Determine top-performing products.
* Evaluate category and regional profitability.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

## Business Insights

Technology generated the highest revenue.

East region showed strong profitability.

Revenue demonstrated seasonal fluctuations.

A small number of products contributed significantly to overall sales.

Top customers accounted for a large share of revenue.

Regional performance indicated opportunities for targeted marketing.



## Recommendations
